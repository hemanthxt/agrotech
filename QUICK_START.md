## ğŸ¯ Common Tasks

### 1. "How much will it cost to grow wheat on 10 acres?"
1. Set crop: **Wheat**
2. Set area: **10 acres**
3. Go to **Finance** tab
4. See total cost breakdown

### 2. "How much water do I need?"
1. Go to **Irrigation** tab
2. Enter temperature, humidity, rainfall
3. Select growth stage
4. Get daily/weekly water requirements

### 3. "When should I plant and harvest?"
1. Go to **Calendar** tab
2. Select planting date
3. Get complete timeline with activities

### 4. "How much fertilizer do I need?"
1. Go to **Fertilizer** tab
2. Enter soil test (optional)
3. Choose organic/chemical
4. Get exact quantities and costs

### 5. "What crop should I grow next year?"
1. Go to **Rotation** tab
2. Enter current crop
3. Get 3-year rotation plan with benefits

### 6. "Will I make a profit?"
1. Go to **Finance** tab
2. Enter expected yield
3. Enter selling price
4. Get profit/loss and ROI
